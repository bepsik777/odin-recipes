Space Cookies

Here we will cover an easy space cookies recipe. Although it is sweet and may be considered a desert, we sugest you eat it before the main dish, as an appetizer. It might help you enjoy the rest of your meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cannabutter
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 and 1/3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/3 cups of chocolate chips
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
