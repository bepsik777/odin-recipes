Roquefort Pasta Recipe

Roquefort pasta

This is a delicious and very simple recipe, for pasta with sauce made out of french blue cheese called Roquefort. If you ate the space cookies from my recipe your mind will be blown after trying this.

Ingredients

  • 60 g Roquefort cheese
  • 200 g pasta
  • 150 ml cream
  • 100 g Serrano ham
  • 30 g nuts
