Roquefort Pasta Recipe

Roquefort pasta

This is a delicious and very simple recipe, for pasta with sauce made out of french blue cheese called Roquefort. If you ate the space cookies from my recipe your mind will be blown after trying this.

Ingredients

  • 60 g Roquefort cheese
  • 200 g pasta
  • 150 ml cream
  • 100 g Serrano ham
  • 30 g nuts

Steps

  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Cook time: 5 minutes
  • Servings: 2
  1. Melt the Roquefort cheese with the cream in a pan over low heat. At the same time, bake the fresh pasta in boiling salted water.
  2. Pour the drained pasta into the cheese sauce and serve it in the plates. Then add some slices of Serrano ham and the crushed nuts. Enjoy!
